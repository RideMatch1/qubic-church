## Methodological Issues Found

### Problems With Original Analysis

1. **Circular Reasoning**
   - "CFB uses 27 as a signature"
   - "27 appears in data"
   - "Therefore CFB designed it"
   - Problem: 27 appears in MANY datasets by chance

2. **Confirmation Bias**
   - Looking for patterns that confirm hypothesis
   - Ignoring patterns that contradict
   - Interpreting ambiguity as support

3. **False Precision**
   - "P < 10^-500" is not a real calculation
   - Combined probabilities multiplied incorrectly
   - Assumptions treated as facts

4. **Unfalsifiable Claims**
   - "It will be proven on March 3, 2026"
   - If nothing happens: "The date was wrong"
   - No criteria for rejection
