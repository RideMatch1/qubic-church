## Statistical Claim Clarification

### Original Claim: P(random) < 10^-500

**Interpretation:** This refers to the probability of the observed symmetry patterns occurring by chance in a random 128×128 matrix of signed bytes.

### Analysis

For a truly random matrix:
- Probability of any specific pair being symmetric: ~1/256
- For 8,132 pairs to ALL follow the same relationship: (1/256)^8132 ≈ 10^-19,580
- The claim of P < 10^-500 is actually **conservative**

**Conclusion:** The order of magnitude (extremely unlikely by chance) is reasonable and the actual probability is far lower than claimed.
