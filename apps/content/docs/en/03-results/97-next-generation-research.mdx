### Statistical Validation

In 100,000 Monte Carlo simulations with random Qubic address pairs:
- Individual properties each have p between 0.001 and 0.134
- **Combined**: 0 out of 100,000 random pairs matched all properties simultaneously
- Estimated combined p = 2.05e-8 (1 in 48.8 million)

This is the **only finding in the entire Bitcoin-Qubic Bridge research** that survives Bonferroni correction.

1. **The exact combined p-value derivation** is not documented in a single script -- individual components are tested in separate scripts

3. **Who constructed these addresses** is unknown -- CFB is the most likely candidate based on access to the matrix

The POCC+HASV pattern is consistent with **deliberate construction** by someone with intimate knowledge of the Anna Matrix.
