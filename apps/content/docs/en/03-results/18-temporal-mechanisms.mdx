## 11. Assessment and Limitations

### 11.1 Tiered Summary

**Tier 1 -- Verified**:
Pre-Genesis timestamp (Sep 10, 2008); Genesis Block timestamp (Jan 3, 2009);
115-day interval; September 10 anniversary publications; modular arithmetic
results; April 2027 timer in Anna Lab code; CFB's documented statements.

**Tier 2 -- Observed**:
CFB's February 5 physical model for informational time travel; structural
parallels between January 2026 experiments and CFB's model; differential block
accessibility in the Anna query interface.

**Tier 3 -- Speculative**:
17.5-year cycle significance; existence of any time-lock mechanism; physical
possibility of informational time travel; LENR as temporal enabler; causal
connection between experiments and CFB's framework; April 2027 producing a
verifiable temporal phenomenon.

### 11.2 Methodological Limitations

1. **Post-hoc pattern selection**: All temporal patterns were identified after
   the data was known. No prediction was registered before observation. This
   fundamentally limits the statistical significance of any finding.

2. **Confirmation bias**: When searching large datasets for temporal patterns,
   the probability of finding *some* apparently significant pattern is high,
   even in random data. Multiple comparison corrections (Bonferroni, etc.) have
   not been applied.

3. **Non-independence**: Several of the "converging" calculations are derived
   from the same underlying data points. Independent analysis has shown that
   claimed "six independent proofs" reduce to approximately three independent
   pieces of information.

4. **Unfalsifiable framing**: Some claims are structured such that non-occurrence
   of predicted events is explained as "not yet time" rather than treated as
   disconfirming evidence. This weakens the scientific status of the hypotheses.

5. **Source verification gaps**: Some February 2026 Discord quotes lack Discord
   Message ID verification. These should be treated as provisional.
