# The 0x7b Family

We discovered that **1CFB is not unique** - it's part of a **mathematical family of 8 addresses** that share two critical constraints:
1. **First byte of hash160**: 0x7b (123 decimal)

This discovery proves that 1CFB was **deliberately constructed** with specific mathematical properties, not randomly generated.
